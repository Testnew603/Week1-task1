Welcome to Florence Marble and Stone Company. Here you'll meet highly experienced master craftsmen, who are passionate about stone-craft and enthusiastic about sharing their expertise to help you manage every aspect of your project better.


At Florence Marble & Stone Company, we understand that our rich natural heritage has given us abundance, which is why we are dedicated to sourcing the finest materials from all over the world to enhance the natural beauty of your home. Uncompromising on quality, Florence Marble & Stone Company will always endeavor to deliver amazing marble and stone that are amongst nature’s greatest gifts to mankind. For more information about our products and services, please visit our showroom. You may also contact us to arrange a consultation with one of our expert representatives.
